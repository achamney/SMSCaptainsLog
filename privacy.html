SMS Captain's Log
All messages sent from the user's phone, from the web app, and sent to the user's phone are uploaded to the cloud and encrypted using AES encryption. This encryption relies on a passcode entered by the user when first starting the app. The security and safety of your data is based on the strength of this passcode. Use SMS Captain's Log at your own risk.
